1. What is the address of the company's corporate headquarters?

Jl. K.H. Zainul Arifin No. 20 Jakarta 11140, Indonesia
Phone : (62-21) 6333 4838, 633 4848, 633 4861
Fax : (62-21) 633 3080
PO BOX 1119 JKT

2. Where is the company traded and what is its ticker?

The company is traded at Indonesia Stock Exchange under the symbol PGAS

3. When was PGN's initial public offering? What was the issue price?

We listed on December 15th, 2003 at Rp1,500 per share. Stock split 5:1 on August 2008

4. What is PGN shareholder composition?

Currently 56.96% owned by the Republic of Indonesia, 43.04% owned by public
The Republic of Indonesia owned 56.96% and one Dwiwarna Share with special voting rights

5. What is PGN's dividend policy

The IPO prospectus states that PGN’s policy on the payment of dividends is 50% of net income.
However, in the last 5 years,the average dividend payout ratio has been 62.73%.
