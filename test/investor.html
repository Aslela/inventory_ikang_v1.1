CORPORATE PROFILE

As a publicly listed company, and in line with our aspiration to be world class, PGN fully supports the principles of disclosure and transparency in all our activities, particularly those of a material nature, and delivers balanced, factual information promptly through email, conference calls, briefings and reports to the general and specialist media, and here on our website.

As well as submitting reports to Bapepam-LK and the Indonesia Stock Exchange (IDX), PGN regularly engages analysts and investors through forums such as public presentations, analysts' meetings, analysts' visits and site visits, as well as participating in conferences and road shows.
